## About this component 🎛️
Displays a spiral pattern of images or Framer components, revolving around a central area. Images smoothly rotate in 3D and fade to create a sense of depth.

## Key Features ✨
- Users can choose between displaying images or linked items
- Fine-grained control over appearance, spiral geometry, and animation speed
- The fade-in and fade-out effects add depth and focus to the gallery
- Highly customizable spiral layout with smooth animation and depth effects
- Supports both static images and dynamic Framer components as spiral items
